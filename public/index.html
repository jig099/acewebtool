La Jolla is a smart seaside area, known for its rugged coastline. Trails wind past pine trees and sandstone canyons in Torrey Pines State Natural Reserve, while the 2 courses at world-renowned Torrey Pines Golf Course sit atop sea cliffs. La Jolla Cove has sweeping ocean views, and the Shell Beach tide pools are home to anemones and crabs. Posh boutiques and waterfront restaurants dot walkable La Jolla Village.

Herringbone in La jolla

Experience the Flagship in the Coastal Community of La Jolla Herringbone features a “Fish Meats Field” coastal cuisine with an emphasis on line-caught seafood and high-quality meats. As a delightful hem in the Fabric of Social Dining, Herringbone La Jolla takes guests on an ocean-to-table culinary excursion and social dining experience with al fresco dining, a raw bar, exhibition kitchen and 100-year-old olive trees in the famed beach community.
